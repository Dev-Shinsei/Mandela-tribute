Nelson Mandela

The first black President of South Africa

Nelson Mandela Time Line

  • 1918 - July 18 - Born at Mvezo in the Transkei
  • 1955 - Watches as the Congress of the People at Kliptown adopts the Freedom Charter
  • 1956 - Arrested and later joins 155 others on trial for teason. All are acquitted by 29 March 1961
  • 1990 - Elected ANC Deputy President
  • 1993 - Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize with President FW de Klerk
  • 1994 - May 9 -Elected by Parliament as first president of a democratic South Africa
  • 1994 - May 10 - Inaugurated as President of the Republic of South Africa
  • 2008 - Asks future generations to continue the fight for social justice
  • 2010 - Formally presented with the Fifa World Cup trophy before it embarks on a tour of South África
  • 2013 - Passes away at home in Johannesburg
